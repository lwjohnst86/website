Luke Johnston is a Postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Public Health in Aarhus University, Denmark. His research is in diabetes epidemiology where he uses data to try to understand how and why individuals develop type 2 diabetes. He's other work includes creating R packages and workflows to make it easier to do data analysis and reproducible research. Almost all of his code for his projects is on GitHub. Teaching proper coding skills and fostering reproducible research is something he is passionate about, especially considering that much of science relies on the results from these codes and analyses.
